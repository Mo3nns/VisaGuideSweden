Welcome To Ultimate Visa Guide For Sweden

Here we have collected guides on the most common visas for Sweden. We have only added the visas and permits that me and my wife have experience with. If you can't find what you are looking for than that means we haven't applied for those and cannot make a guide. We wanted to give you an easy experience applying for a visa and get the most of your questions out of the way. Down below you will find the visas and permits that we have guides for. There might come more in the future, but at the moment we only have experience with the one's below. We strive to keep our website up-to-date, but due to our full-time jobs, we may not always be able to immediately reflect any recent changes or major laws on our website. For this reason, we encourage our visitors to double-check the latest information on the official immigration website. To help you stay informed, we include a date on every page for each guide, indicating when the information was added. We also ensure that the date is updated each time we make changes to our website.
