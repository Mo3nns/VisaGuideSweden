Schengen UT-Extension

"The Ultimate step by step Guide to apply for a VISA to Sweden"

Last update: 2023-03-01

It's possible to extend your visa in Sweden if you have a valid reason, such as if your partner is ill or if you are unable to leave the country. However, I am unable to provide detailed instructions on how to do this as I have not personally done so before.

But I have extended a VISA using the UT extension. If you decide to extend your visa, one option is to use the UT extension, which may make it easier to get approval. However, it's important to keep in mind that extending your visa too many times may cause issues for future visa applications. The embassy may become suspicious and reject future applications unless you can provide a good reason for not extending your visa again. This is something to consider before making the decision to extend your visa.

You can submit an extension application online on the Swedish immigration website or in-person at an immigration office. Keep in mind that extending a visa can carry risks and may affect your future visa applications.

Schengen VISA UT-Extension

  • Documents required
  • Application for visitor's residence permit
  • Copy of passport all pages with content
  • Power of attorney (if someone else represents you)
  • Copy of the ID card of the person you are visiting
  • Means of support in connection with visiting Sweden 255011
  • Copy of paid travel insurance document
  • Document explaining travel plans

Application for visitor's residence permit

Fill out the Application for visitor's residence permit 160511 , if you need help completing the application please click on this Link. On all the application links, we have made an example document where you can find inspiration on how to exactly fill out the forms so it's easier for you.

Power of attorney (if needed)

If you need someone else to help you fill out and submit your visa application, you will need an authorization document called power of attorney.This document gives the person you're appointing permission to act on your behalf. You can find guidance on how to fill out this document by following the link provided below.
Power of attorney 10711 Template

This document can be avoided if you hand in the application by yourself.

Means of support in connection with visiting sweden

If you are being financially supported by the person inviting you to Sweden or have a sponsor, you will need to fill out this form Means of support in connection with visiting Sweden 255011. If you need help with this form, a blueprint is available by following the link provided below.
Means of support in connection with visiting Sweden Template

Don't forget to make a copy of the Sponsors ID-card or Passport.

Passport

For this step, you will need a valid passport that is less than 10 years old and has at least three months of validity remaining beyond the date you plan to leave the Schengen area. You will need to scan all the pages of your passport, including any previous Schengen stamps. If you can't find your old passport, don't worry, you can explain that to the consulate when you submit your application. They will only ask for it if necessary.

Insurance

It's important to have valid travel insurance when applying for a permit to Sweden. Keep in mind that the cheapest option may not always be the best. We recommend using ERV insurance, which offers a good price and a full refund if your visa application is denied. Another reputable option is AXA-Schengen. insurance, But if you choose any other insurance company, please make sure that they cover costs of at least 30,000 Euros. You will need to show proof that you have a valid insurance when you hand in the application.

Reason for Extension

When applying for an extension of your visa in Sweden, it's important to provide a clear and valid reason for why you want to stay longer. This reason should be included in the application. If you want to provide additional information, you can write it down on a separate document and submit it along with your application.

It's also important to include details about your plans during your extended stay, such as travel plans, accommodation, and other activities. This will help the Swedish immigration understand your purpose for staying longer and make a decision on your application.

Processing time

According to the Swedish immigration's website, 75% that apply will get a decision within 4 months. One thing that can help you to get a decision faster is to make a solid application. If the immigration has questions, the processing time could take longer. If you want to see the latest information regarding the processing time, please visit Migrationsverket.se
